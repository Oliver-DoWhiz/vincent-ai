Positioning

Vincent is built like an editorial object, not a cheerful quiz toy.

The mood is luxurious, slightly surgical, and emotionally literate. Users do not get flattered into vague positivity. They get a memorable frame for how they move through work, intimacy, power, and self-control.

Why this can work

  • Fast completion lowers abandonment and supports sharing.
  • Sharper language increases recall and discussion value.
  • Dimension-based scoring gives an explainable structure.
  • Shadow archetypes create tension without pretending to diagnose.

System

The model is simple enough to ship, rich enough to expand.

Signal

Each question scores one dimension from low to high. Vincent currently tracks ambition, boundary-setting, visibility, intensity, discipline, meaning-making, attachment, and initiative.

Mapping

The assessment converts totals into an 8-part signature and matches users to the nearest archetype profile. That keeps the engine interpretable while still feeling nuanced.

Guardrails

Results are framed as reflective insight, not clinical judgment. Vincent can evolve toward mental-health support, but only with evidence, safety flows, and claim discipline.
