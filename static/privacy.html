Privacy and scope

Clear boundaries are part of the product.

Vincent ADHD is a self-report screening and visit-preparation tool for adults. It is not a diagnosis, treatment plan, medication recommendation, therapy service, or crisis service.

What Vincent is

  • An adult-only ADHD-related screening summary.
  • A local-first tool that keeps answers in the browser by default.
  • A way to organize examples and questions for a licensed clinician.

What Vincent is not

  • Not a diagnosis or a way to confirm DSM-5 criteria.
  • Not therapy, crisis support, or medication guidance.
  • Not a clinically validated or licensed screening instrument in this version.

Privacy behavior

Your answers are processed in your browser. They are not sent to Vincent servers. If you choose to save progress, answers are stored locally on this device and can be cleared at any time.

Safety boundary

Vincent includes a minimal safety screen. If someone reports immediate danger or worry about harming themselves or someone else, Vincent interrupts the screening and directs them to emergency or crisis resources.

In the U.S., call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Outside the U.S., contact local emergency services or local crisis resources.
