How it works

Deterministic scoring, local report generation, careful language.

Vincent uses original self-report prompts about recent attention and executive-function patterns, restlessness or impulsivity, functional impairment, childhood history, cross-setting evidence, and overlapping factors worth discussing with a clinician.

01 Current signals

Attention, executive-function, restlessness, and impulsivity are scored in-browser.

02 Context checks

Impairment, childhood history, cross-setting patterns, and overlap factors add context.

03 Cautious result

Vincent returns a screening signal, not a diagnosis, and builds a local report.

Important limitations

Vincent does not diagnose ADHD, provide treatment advice, or claim clinical validation. Other factors such as sleep, anxiety, depression, trauma, substance use, medical issues, or burnout can overlap with ADHD-like symptoms and should be discussed with a licensed clinician.
