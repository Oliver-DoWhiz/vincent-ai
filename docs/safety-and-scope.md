# Vincent ADHD Safety and Scope

## Adult-only scope

Vincent ADHD is for adults 18 and older.

Users must confirm:

- they are 18 or older
- Vincent does not diagnose ADHD or any other condition
- Vincent does not provide therapy, crisis support, medication guidance, or treatment advice
- answers stay in the browser unless the user chooses to export, print, copy, or save locally

## What Vincent does not do

Vincent does not:

- diagnose ADHD
- confirm or rule out DSM-5 criteria
- replace a licensed clinician
- provide therapy
- provide crisis counseling
- recommend medication
- recommend against medication
- provide a treatment plan

## Crisis boundary

Vincent includes a minimal safety question:

- "Are you currently in immediate danger or worried you may harm yourself or someone else?"

If the user answers yes, Vincent interrupts the normal screening flow and shows crisis guidance instead of continuing.

Required crisis language:

- If you are in immediate danger or worried you may harm yourself or someone else, call emergency services now.
- In the U.S., call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
- Outside the U.S., contact local emergency services or local crisis resources.

## Overlapping factors

Vincent may surface factors that can overlap with ADHD-like symptoms and are worth discussing with a clinician, including:

- sleep problems
- possible sleep apnea signs
- anxiety symptoms
- depressive symptoms
- burnout or chronic stress
- trauma or PTSD symptoms
- periods of unusually high energy with reduced need for sleep or risky behavior
- substance use
- autism-related rigidity, if relevant
- learning difficulties
- medical factors such as thyroid issues, anemia, chronic pain, or medication side effects

Vincent must not claim that these factors explain or rule out ADHD. They are prompts for clinician discussion only.

## Validation and licensing boundary

Vincent v0 uses original, non-diagnostic self-report items. It does not claim:

- standardized scale licensing
- clinical validation
- psychometric validity
- medical screening authorization

## Required disclaimer

Vincent must state clearly:

"This is a self-report screening summary to help you decide whether to seek formal evaluation. It is not a diagnosis, treatment plan, medication recommendation, or substitute for professional evaluation."
